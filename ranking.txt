Ranks is an array

This can be solo rank or many depending on your choices

ranks => [
'silver',
'gold',
'platinum'
]

categorize a user by rank based on amount
each rank has min and max amount needed
i.e.
silver min: 1 max: 999
gold min: 1,000 max: 9,999
platinum min: 10,000 max: 100,000

categorized commission on a user base on rank
we give commission for each rank i.e. Fix Value
silver 50
gold 100
platinum 1000
